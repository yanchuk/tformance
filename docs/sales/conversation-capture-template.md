## Aggregation Questions (Review After 10+ Conversations)

After enough conversations, look for patterns:

1. **What pain came up most often?** (Count by category above)
2. **What feature was requested most?** (Count from requests table)
3. **What was the most common objection?**
4. **What's the real ICP?** (Compare who showed genuine demand vs. who you thought would)
5. **What should the wedge be?** (Based on what they'd pay for, not what you want to build)
6. **Are the strategy docs right?** (Compare premises against conversation data)
