# Writing Well: Extended Examples

This file provides additional before/after transformations and SaaS-specific copywriting patterns for deeper reference.

---

## Landing Page Copy Transformations

### Hero Sections

**Example 1: The Feature Dump**

Before:
> Tformance is a comprehensive engineering analytics platform designed to help
> CTOs and engineering managers gain visibility into their team's performance
> through advanced AI-powered analysis of code commits, pull requests, and
> development workflows across GitHub, Jira, and Slack integrations.

After:
> Is AI helping your team or slowing them down?
> Find out in 30 seconds.

**Example 2: The Buzzword Salad**

Before:
> Leverage AI-driven insights to optimize your engineering velocity and achieve
> best-in-class performance metrics through our industry-leading platform.

After:
> See exactly where your team's time goes.
> Then fix it.

**Example 3: The Vague Promise**

Before:
> Transform your development process with actionable data that drives results
> and empowers your team to reach new heights of productivity.

After:
> Ship 40% faster. We'll show you how.

## Feature Descriptions

### Bad: Feature-Focused

> **GitHub Integration**
> Our platform provides bidirectional synchronization with GitHub repositories,
> enabling real-time data ingestion of commits, pull requests, and code reviews
> for comprehensive analysis.

### Good: Benefit-Focused

> **GitHub Integration**
> Connect once. We pull in everything—commits, PRs, reviews—automatically.
> No setup. No maintenance.

---

### Bad: Technical Jargon

> **AI Detection Engine**
> Our proprietary machine learning algorithms analyze code patterns to detect
> AI-assisted development with 95% accuracy using natural language processing
> and commit analysis heuristics.

### Good: Plain Benefits

> **AI Detection**
> We spot AI-written code automatically. No manual tagging needed.

### Error Messages

**Authentication Error**

Before:
> Error: Authentication failed. Invalid credentials provided.
> Error code: AUTH_401

After:
> Wrong password.
> [Try again] or [Reset password]

---

**Connection Error**

Before:
> Error: Unable to establish connection to remote server.
> Please check your network connectivity and try again.

After:
> Can't connect right now.
> Check your internet and try again.
> [Retry]

---

**Rate Limit Error**

Before:
> Error: Rate limit exceeded. Too many requests have been made.
> Please wait before retrying your request.

After:
> Slow down! Too many requests.
> Try again in a minute.

---

**Permission Error**

Before:
> Error: You do not have sufficient permissions to perform this action.
> Please contact your administrator for access.

After:
> You don't have access to this.
> Ask a team admin to add you.

## Headlines and Taglines

### Pattern: Problem → Solution

- **Problem:** "Don't know if AI tools are helping?"
- **Solution:** "Now you will."

### Pattern: Benefit → How

- **Benefit:** "Ship faster."
- **How:** "See what's slowing you down."

### Pattern: Question → Promise

- **Question:** "How much of your code is AI-generated?"
- **Promise:** "We'll tell you—and whether it matters."

## Writing Exercises

### Exercise 1: Cut 50%

Take this 50-word paragraph and cut it to 25:

> Our innovative platform leverages state-of-the-art artificial intelligence
> technology to analyze your engineering team's development workflow patterns
> and provide actionable, data-driven insights that enable engineering leaders
> to make better decisions about resource allocation and process optimization.

**Target:**
> Our AI analyzes how your team works and shows you where to improve.
> (14 words)

### Exercise 2: Active Voice

Convert these passive sentences:

- "Metrics are calculated daily by our system" →
- "Alerts will be sent when thresholds are exceeded" →
- "PRs can be filtered by date range" →

**Answers:**
- "Our system calculates metrics daily"
- "We'll alert you when you exceed thresholds"
- "Filter PRs by date range"

### Exercise 3: Kill the Jargon

Rewrite without buzzwords:

> Leverage our best-in-class analytics to optimize your engineering velocity
> and drive transformational outcomes across your organization.

**Target:**
> See where your team is slow. Then fix it.

## Quick Reference Card

### Words to Cut

| Cut | Keep |
|-----|------|
| utilize | use |
| leverage | use |
| facilitate | help |
| optimize | improve |
| implement | do, build |
| functionality | feature |
| in order to | to |
| due to the fact that | because |
| at this point in time | now |
| in the event that | if |

### Sentence Starters to Avoid

- "It is important to note that..."
- "It should be mentioned that..."
- "It goes without saying that..."
- "As you may know..."
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "At the end of the day..."

### Strong Verbs for CTAs

| Action | Verb |
|--------|------|
| Create account | Sign up, Join, Start |
| View more | See, Explore, Discover |
| Purchase | Get, Buy, Unlock |
| Learn | Read, Watch, Try |
| Submit | Send, Share, Post |
